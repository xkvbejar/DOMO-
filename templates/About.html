ABOUT THE SYSTEM

DoMo (Dog Monitoring System) is a system that allows you to see your dog’s behavior on a timeline. Our mission is to help dog owners with their pets. We prioritize the wellness of your dogs as they’re also part of the family. The application is machine learning-driven to monitor your dog’s behavior. DoMo (Dog Monitoring System), has the ability to detect specific behaviors,  particularly when the dogs are left unattended in their homes.



Try out DoMo, if you are a dog owner, you will not regret it!
